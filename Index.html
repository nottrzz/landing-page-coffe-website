Our story

More than coffee, it's an experience

Nestled in the highlands of Sapit, East Lombok, Kopi Sapit is a family-owned coffee sanctuary. We cultivate, roast, and brew our own Typica & Arabica Single Origin beans, grown on volcanic slopes.

From the moment you step onto our terrace, you’ll witness a stunning panorama — the endless blue sea meeting the silhouette of Sumbawa island. Cozy interior, warm service, and the aroma of freshly ground coffee define our soul.
